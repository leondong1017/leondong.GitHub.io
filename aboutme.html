Introduction

Hi, I am Leon Dong, UX Designer born and raised in Shanghai.

During my graduate studies, I have been working with my supervisor in the field of intelligent and interaction design, and I have learned and practiced design methodologies including design thinking, service design, and experience design in various university-enterprise collaborative course projects. Thanks to the multidisciplinary background of Shanghai Jiao Tong University, I often collaborate with students from different majors in interdisciplinary projects, deepening my vertical design skills while cultivating my horizontal skills of teamwork and team management.

The main reason why I chose the field of experience design is that many companies have various experience problems in the process of rapid growth, and I have strong empathy for user insight, and I can optimize the product and experience by considering the commercial feasibility, technical viability and design innovation. My past experience in multi-discipline projects also enables me to improve user experience with different solution.
